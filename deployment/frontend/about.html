About ClariCare

An ethically designed AI health guidance platform — helping people understand their symptoms and find the right care without ever replacing a real doctor.

Our Mission

Clarity. Not Diagnosis.

ClariCare was built on a simple belief: people deserve clear, calm, and responsible guidance about their health — especially before they can see a doctor.

We use state-of-the-art NLP to extract symptoms from natural language and guide users to the right type of specialist, without ever making a clinical diagnosis.

Under the Hood

How the AI Works

A 4-stage pipeline processes every conversation — from raw text to structured health guidance.

1. NLP Preprocessing

Tokenization, lemmatization, and stopword removal via NLTK.

2. BioBERT Matching

Semantic similarity with pre-computed biomedical embeddings (threshold 0.93).

3. Risk Classification

Rule-based classifier assigns Low / Medium / High risk based on symptoms.

4. Response Generation

Structured narrative with specialist recommendations and lifestyle suggestions.

Our Commitment

Ethical by Design

What ClariCare Does

  • ✓ Extracts symptoms from natural language
  • ✓ Classifies symptoms by clinical risk level
  • ✓ Recommends appropriate specialist types
  • ✓ Provides evidence-based lifestyle suggestions
  • ✓ Alerts users to high-risk symptom combinations
  • ✓ Always displays a clear medical disclaimer

What ClariCare Never Does

  • ✗ Make a clinical diagnosis
  • ✗ Prescribe medications or treatments
  • ✗ Replace professional medical consultation
  • ✗ Store personal health data
  • ✗ Guarantee accuracy of AI assessments
  • ✗ Handle medical emergencies (call 112 instead)

⚕️ Medical Disclaimer

ClariCare is an AI-powered guidance tool and does NOT provide medical diagnoses, treatment plans, or prescriptions. The information provided is for general informational purposes only. Always consult a qualified healthcare professional for proper medical evaluation and advice. If you are experiencing a medical emergency, please call your local emergency number immediately.
